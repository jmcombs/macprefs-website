### Can I use the Free tier long-term?

Absolutely. macprefs Free is a complete tool for managing Apple preferences. It's not a trial â€” use it as long as you want.

### What happens when I get a new Mac?

Deactivate your license on the old Mac (`macprefs license deactivate`), then activate on the new one. Your license key never expires.

### How do I activate my license?

After purchase, you'll receive a license key via email. Run `macprefs license activate --key <key>` and Pro features unlock immediately.

### What payment methods are accepted?

Payments are handled securely by [Lemon Squeezy](https://www.lemonsqueezy.com). They accept credit/debit cards, PayPal, and other regional payment methods.

### Is this a subscription?

No. macprefs Pro is a **one-time purchase** of $39. No recurring fees, no annual renewals.

### What about third-party app domains?

Third-party app domain support is a planned feature for a future release. Currently, macprefs manages Apple preference domains (`com.apple.*` and `NSGlobalDomain`). We'll announce third-party domain support when it's available.
