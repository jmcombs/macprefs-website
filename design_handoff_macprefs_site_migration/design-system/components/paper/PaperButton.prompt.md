**PaperButton** — Paper Kit's rounded pill button in Netservant colors. Full-radius, uppercase, soft "paper" lift on hover. Use as the primary marketing CTA; pair a `fill` with a `neutral` or `outline`.

```jsx
<PaperButton>Get started</PaperButton>
<PaperButton variant="neutral" iconLeft={<i className="codicon codicon-play" />}>Watch video</PaperButton>
<PaperButton variant="outline" accent="peach">Download</PaperButton>
```

Variants: `fill` (solid) · `neutral` (white + paper shadow) · `outline` · `link`. Sizes `sm·md·lg`. `accent` = `path` (Path Blue) or any Latte name. `round` (pill, default), `uppercase`, `block`.
