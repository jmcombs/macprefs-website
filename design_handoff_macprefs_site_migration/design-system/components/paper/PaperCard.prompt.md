**PaperCard** — Paper Kit's soft rounded surface (rounder + softer shadow than the core `Card`). Optional pastel `tint`, top `media` slot, and hover lift. The workhorse container for Paper-style marketing sections.

```jsx
<PaperCard tint="teal" interactive media={<Photo seed="gallery" />}>
  <h4>Beautiful gallery</h4>
  <p>Spend your time generating new ideas.</p>
</PaperCard>
```

Props: `tint` (Latte name), `media`, `raised`, `interactive` (hover lift), `align`, `padding`.
