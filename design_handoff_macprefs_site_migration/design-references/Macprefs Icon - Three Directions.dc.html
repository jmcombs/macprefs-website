Where the @e sits
A · corner mark. A ©-sized @e in the lower-right — a quiet signature that never competes with the toggles. Drops at favicon scale, as a copyright mark would.
B · corner mark. The same ©-sized @e in white, riding the lower corner of the glass. Present but unobtrusive; the favicon keeps the switch only.
C · corner mark. White @e in the same © spot on the flat tile. The bold toggle pair carries the icon; the @e just signs it.
